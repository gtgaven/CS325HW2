Stick Dude wished for a Nuclear Winter

  • High Concept: A sidescroller which players stay alive by being in the right position on the screen.
  • Player Experience Goals: have a fun time with hardly any sort of time investment.
  • Genre: 2D sidescroller
  • Game play: The player loses health from being exposed to the elements and needs to regain health by using the protecting waves from the ufo. BUT, if the ufo captures stick dude, the game also ends.
  • Prototype goal: Modify HW1 with emphasising an intense player experience.
  • Features: The fact that the only way to get health is also a threat will cause the player to constantly struggle between life and death.
  • Setting/Premise: The aliens have invaded the world and have started the nuclear apocalypse.
  • Story: Stick dude is forever trapped in a nuclear wasteland and must use the invading UFO for protection while avoiding it simultaneously.
  • Target Audience: casual gamers of all ages.
  • Play time: ~5 minutes
  • Strategies: since you can move backwards faster than forwards, players will most likely utilize that to their advantage.
  • Prototype Assets: All visual assets were made from scratch using MSPaint and Photoshop. Audio assets were taken from http://phaser.io/examples/v2/audio/loop
